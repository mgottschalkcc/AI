Conceptual vs. Theoretical

Conceptual Framework: A structure that organizes and guides the research process by identifying key variables, concepts, and relationships.

Theoretical Framework: A set of established theories or perspectives that guide your approach to the research.

Video Overview

Dr. Elizabeth Yardley breaks down the differences between these frameworks and how they connect to your broader research paradigm.
