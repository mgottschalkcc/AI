Video Tutorial: Conceptual vs. Theoretical

Dr. Elizabeth Yardley breaks down the differences between these frameworks and how they connect to your broader research paradigm.

🎯 Conceptual Framework

A structure that organizes and guides the research process by identifying key variables, concepts, and relationships based on your specific study context.

🔭 Theoretical Framework

A set of established theories or perspectives from existing literature that guide your approach to interpreting the research problem.
